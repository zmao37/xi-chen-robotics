About

陈晰 Xi Chen

Building intelligent robotic systems that generalize across embodiments, tasks, and real-world environments.

I am a Researcher at the Robotics Lab, Beijing Institute for General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI). My research focuses on embodied AI, robot learning, and multi-robot systems, developing data-driven methods for cross-robot adaptation, robust policy learning from heterogeneous data, and coordination in heterogeneous multi-robot systems.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Tsinghua University (IIIS), where I conducted research on cross-robot imitation learning, offline reinforcement learning, and policy learning from heterogeneous data. I received my Ph.D. from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden.

My long-term goal is to enable transferable and deployable robot intelligence that can learn from collective experience, share knowledge across heterogeneous robots, and operate reliably in complex real-world settings.

I welcome collaboration inquiries from researchers and students interested in embodied AI, robot learning, and multi-robot systems.
